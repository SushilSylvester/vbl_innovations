Sourcing

Techniques of Manufacturing

Reimborde

In this process there is a lot of craftsmanship involved. In the production of a Remborde (or fully-wrapped) strap, the upper leather is turned arond the breathable natural cotton fleece. glued and finally moulded. The strap is very stable, soft and durable.

Semi Reimborde

In this process the upper leather is pulled to the bottom line of the lining leather (not-underneath) and is sealed there. The gluing is what holds the strap together, the seam is only for the decoration.

Cut Edge

The leather is punched out or cut into the shape of the strap and then glued. Then the open edge are sealed with color.
